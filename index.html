5-Minute Breathing Exercise

  1. Find a comfortable position
  2. Close your eyes or maintain a soft gaze
  3. Breathe in for 4 counts
  4. Hold for 4 counts
  5. Exhale for 4 counts
  6. Repeat for 5 minutes
